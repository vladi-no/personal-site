Solar Plan & Installation Overview

System Overview

Location Vadu Parului, Prahova, Romania
Total System Size 8.08 kW Peak
Estimated Required Roof Area ~32.0 m²
Grid Connection Type Hybrid (Single-Phase with Battery Backup)

Primary Hardware Components

Solar Panels 16 x Trina Solar Vertex S+ 505W
Model: TSM-505-NEG18R.28, N-Type i-TOPCon, Black Frame
Inverter 1 x Deye 8 kW Single-Phase Hybrid
Model: SUN-8K-SG05LP1-EU-SM2 (or AM2-P)
Includes Deye Wi-Fi Module for monitoring
Battery Storage 2 x Deye SE-G5.1 Pro-B Low Voltage (LV)
Total Capacity: 10.24 kWh
Mounting System Aluminum rails, roof hooks (tailored for tile/metal), mid/end clamps

Balance of System (Cabling & Connectors)

  • Battery Cabling: Copper power cables (25 mm² cross-section) for parallel and inverter connection.
  • Communication: RJ45 data cables for BMS link between batteries and inverter.
  • PV Cabling (DC): UV-resistant red and black solar cable (4 mm² or 6 mm²).
  • Grounding: Yellow-green copper grounding cable (16 mm²) for metal structure and panels.
  • Connectors: MC4 connector sets (male/female) for panel series and inverter connection.
  • Cable Management: UV-resistant flexible conduits (copex) for roof routing, flame-retardant plastic cable trays for wall organization, copper ring terminals, and pin terminals.

Protections & Switchgear

  • DC Protection (Panels): 1 x PV Combiner Box equipped with Surge Protection Devices (SPD) and DC fuses.
  • DC Protection (Battery): 1 x Main DC Disconnect / High-power automatic breaker (e.g., 200A) for the battery-to-inverter circuit.
  • AC Protection: 1 x AC Protection Box with automatic breakers for grid input and backup load output.
  • Isolation: 1 x Manual Transfer Switch (3-position: Grid / Off / Inverter) to safely isolate the house during maintenance or blackouts.

Texas Hold’em Poker Hands

♠ ♥ ♦ ♣

Best 5-card hand out of 7 cards (2 hole + 5 community). Rankings shown worst → best.

Card order: A K Q J 10 9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2 Ace-low straight: A-2-3-4-5 Suits: no ranking (ties can split)

Individual Card Values (High → Low)

A KQJ 10987 65432

Ace is usually high, but can be low only in the straight A-2-3-4-5.

Suit Colors

Suits don’t rank in Texas Hold’em — color is just for readability.

♠ Spades ♥ Hearts ♦ Diamonds ♣ Clubs

If two players make the exact same 5-card hand, the pot is split.

Hand Rankings (Worst → Best)

  1. 1

    High Card

    No pair / straight / flush

    Your best hand is simply the highest cards.

    Example: A♠ Q♦ 9♣ 6♥ 3♠
  2. 2

    One Pair

    2 cards same rank

    Two cards match; remaining cards are kickers.

    Example: A♦ A♣ 9♣ 5♥ 2♦
  3. 3

    Two Pair

    2 different pairs

    Two separate pairs + one kicker.

    Example: J♣ J♥ 4♠ 4♦ 9♣
  4. 4

    Three of a Kind

    Trips / Set

    Three cards match; plus two kickers.

    Example: 8♠ 8♦ 8♣ K♥ 2♠
  5. 5

    Straight

    5 in sequence

    Five consecutive ranks (suits don’t matter).

    Example: 10♠ 9♦ 8♣ 7♠ 6♥ Ace-low: A-2-3-4-5
  6. 6

    Flush

    5 same suit

    Five cards of the same suit (not in sequence).

    Example: A♣ J♣ 9♣ 5♣ 2♣
  7. 7

    Full House

    3 of a kind + a pair

    Three matching cards plus a separate pair.

    Example: Q♠ Q♥ Q♦ 7♣ 7♦
  8. 8

    Four of a Kind

    Quads

    Four cards match; plus one kicker.

    Example: K♣ K♦ K♠ K♥ 3♦
  9. 9

    Straight Flush

    Straight + Flush

    Five in sequence, all same suit.

    Example: 9♦ 8♦ 7♦ 6♦ 5♦
  10. 10

    Royal Flush

    A-K-Q-J-10 same suit

    The best possible hand in Texas Hold’em.

    Example: A♠ K♠ Q♠ J♠ 10♠
Tiebreakers: Same hand type → compare the highest relevant ranks; if still tied, compare kickers. Exact same 5-card hand → split pot.
